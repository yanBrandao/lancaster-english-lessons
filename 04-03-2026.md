# Verb tenses review

## Present simple

Used for 
1. Routine
    a. I wake up at 7:30 am, every day
2. Facts
    a. I work with differents programming languages.
3. Schedules
    a. My english class starts at 3:00 pm.


- To be
- Have got
- Modal verbs
- To be able to

## Past continouos

Used for
1. Action that was happening at a specific time in the past
    a. You were sleeping when I got home.

# Present perfect

Used for
1. An action that started at some time in the past (we don't know when) and is still true now.
    a. I have fixed a security problem at Bose.

We use the present perfect to indicate an action that started in the past and continues to the present and is often used to show a duration of time.

On present perfect we use the HAVE/HAS + PAST PARTICIPLE

Example:
I have worked here for 5 years.
He has done his homework.

# Past perfect

Used for
1. An action that happened at a specific time in the past
    a. You had worked with programming language as well.

On the past perfect we use the HAD + PAST PARTICIPLE

Note: Usually interrupted by a past simple action.

Example:
I had done so many things in this company.
