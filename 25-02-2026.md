# 1 Unit : Grammar - Tense Review

## Pratice

**Exercise 1:** Complete the questions. Change the verb forms if necessary.

**How long / you / sell crème brûlée?**

For over four years. I got started in 2009 in the Mission area of San Francisco.

Answer: How long have you been selling crème brûlée?

*Whats is the verb tense used in the answer?* Present Perfect Continuous

**What / do / before / start your own business?**

It was working as a carpenter.

Answer: What did you do before you start your own business?

*Whats is the verb tense used in the answer?* Simple Past
